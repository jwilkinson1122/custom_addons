Odoo 15: Attachment Thumbnail View (Community)

Display Thumbnail view in attachment. User can preview the attached files in new tab.

Note: This module is tested and working in Odoo community edition but not tested in Enterprise version. And we are supporting Ubuntu OS and not Windows OS.

How to use

  • Here is the thumbnail view of attachment.


  • By clicking on name of record, if file is PDF then it will open in new tab for preview.


  • PDF preview.


  • If file format is other then pdf then it will open this kind of popup to open or download.
