POS Customer's Favorites

With the help of this module, a seller can see the customer's favourite products or products that are frequently purchased by the customer, This module is useful in the following scenario :-

  • A seller wishes to see customer previously purchased products in the selected category at any time in POS.
  • A seller wishes to see all product purchased by customer with product sales details in backend
  • A seller can remind customer if the customer forgets to purchase some products that he usually purchased.
  • A seller can inform the customer if any discount/offer is available in customer's previously purchased product.
  • No need to search products throughout the list of all products, customer favorite product list will show customer's frequently purchased products category wise.

Features
  • Seller can see customer's favourite products or customer's previously purchased products at any time in running session.
  • Seller can see customer's favourite products category-wise.
  • Seller can remove/apply customer's favourite filter at any time in POS in just one click.
  • Seller can see partner wise product sale in backend.
  • Seller can see customer's previously purchased products details/history with product sales in backend.
  • No backend configuration required.
  • POS Session View
  • Backend View
